Privacy Policy

ZERØ · Effective date: 19 June 2026

This policy explains what data ZERØ collects and how it is used. If anything here is unclear, contact us at laliotisnikos4@gmail.com.

ZERØ ("the app", "we", "us") is a salary and tax net-income calculator for Android and iOS. We take your privacy seriously and collect only the data needed to run the app. This policy describes what we collect, why, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in, we collect your email address and name. Authentication is handled through Supabase and, if you choose them, Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple. With Sign in with Apple you may use Apple's Hide My Email feature, in which case we receive only a relay address.
  • Financial figures you enter. Salary, budgets, and related amounts you type into the app are stored only on your device and used to produce your net-income estimates. We never upload, transmit, or receive them, and we do not connect to your bank or import transactions.
  • Purchase information. If you subscribe to ZERØ Pro, your purchase and subscription status is processed through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat so we can unlock and restore your subscription. We do not receive your card or payment details.
  • Crash and diagnostic data. We use Sentry to collect crash reports and error diagnostics so we can fix problems. Sentry is configured with personally identifiable information (PII) disabled (sendDefaultPii=false), so crash reports are not intentionally tagged with your identity.

2. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To calculate and display your salary/tax net-income estimates.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the stability of the app.

3. How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. We rely on the following service providers (sub-processors) strictly to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication and account storage.
  • Google and Apple — sign-in providers, when you use them.
  • Sentry — crash and error diagnostics.
  • Google Play Billing and RevenueCat — subscription processing for ZERØ Pro.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. Crash diagnostics are retained according to Sentry's standard retention windows. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

5. Your rights and data deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account and associated data directly in the app at any time: open Profile → Delete account. You can also email laliotisnikos4@gmail.com and we will process your request. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA.

6. Children

ZERØ is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

8. Contact
