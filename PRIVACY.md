# Privacy Policy — Zero

**Last updated:** 19 June 2026

Zero ("the app", "we") is a personal finance app. This policy explains what
data the app collects, why, and your choices. Contact:
**laliotisnikos4@gmail.com**.

## Summary

- Your **financial data** (budgets, amounts, calculations) is stored **only on
  your device**. We do not upload, transmit, or have access to it.
- We collect the **minimum** needed to run an account, fix crashes, and process
  subscriptions.
- We do **not** sell your data, we do **not** use it for advertising, and we do
  **not** include any advertising or third‑party analytics/tracking SDKs.

## Data we collect

| Data | Why | Where it goes |
|------|-----|---------------|
| Email address | Create and sign in to your account | Supabase (our auth backend) |
| Name (if provided by Google/Apple sign‑in) | Show who is signed in | Supabase |
| Account identifier (user ID) | Link your session to your account | Supabase |
| Crash logs & performance diagnostics | Diagnose and fix bugs | Sentry |
| Purchase / subscription status | Unlock and restore Zero Pro | Google Play & RevenueCat |

Crash and diagnostic data is collected **without** personal identifiers
(`sendDefaultPii` is disabled).

## Data we do NOT collect

- Budgets, transaction amounts, income, and any figures you enter — these stay
  on your device only.
- No location, contacts, photos, advertising ID, or tracking identifiers.

## Service providers (processors)

We use these providers strictly to operate the app. They process data on our
behalf and do not use it for their own purposes:

- **Supabase** — authentication and account storage.
- **Sentry** — crash reporting and diagnostics.
- **RevenueCat** + **Google Play Billing** — subscription management.

## Data security

All data sent off your device is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

## Data retention & deletion

We keep account data for as long as your account exists. You can request
deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing
**laliotisnikos4@gmail.com**; we will delete it within 30 days. On-device data
is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

## Children

Zero is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data
from them.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last
updated" date above.

## Contact

Questions or requests: **laliotisnikos4@gmail.com**
